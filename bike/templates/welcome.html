Let's make it New York city way

A bike sharing system is awesome and we intend to make it even better by plugging in the power of the community.

Get involved right now!

  • Citizen, visitors place the stations where you need them. Share your ideas.
  • Artists or designers, take the opportunity to bring art back to the streets of NY. A bike sharing system means a lot of bike stations. Help us to design the space and create the greatest live outdoor museum of the world.
  • Companies, fundations, take the oportunity to make it real and invent new participative economic developement.
  • Bike specialists, give your idea of the ideal bike for such a system or invent a new kind of maintenance system.
  • Historian, help us to retrace the history of bicycle in Manhattan to root this new system.
  • Botanists, make it green.
